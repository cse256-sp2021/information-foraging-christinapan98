Ages 2 1/2 – 3: Teddy Bears, Panda Bears

The Teddy Bear class is the morning session for 2 1/2 – and 3-year-old children.

The Panda Bear class is the afternoon session for 2 1/2 – and 3-year-old children. In both these classes, teachers design a variety of engaging activities that gradually encourage children to interact and communicate with others. The indoor classroom and outdoor playground are safe, interesting spaces for younger bears to explore and make sense of their world.

  • Student to teacher ratio is 6:1.
  • Children participate in individual, small-group and large-group activities.
  • Teachers and university student workers engage with children throughout the day, encouraging conversation, listening and collaborative play.
  • Classrooms provide high quality, rotating materials that are developmentally appropriate to challenge children’s intellectual growth.
